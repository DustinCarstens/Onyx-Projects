About Onyx

A Workshop for
Thinking Out Loud

I'm Dustin Carstens. Onyx is where I build things — data visualizations, web tools, analytical deep-dives, and whatever else pulls me in. Named after the black stone, it's a space for projects that are polished, intentional, and worth sharing.

I work with AI as a design and development partner — using tools like Claude to accelerate ideation, code implementation, and problem-solving. The vision and direction are mine; AI helps me build faster and explore ideas more deeply. This collaborative approach lets me focus on what matters: creating tools that work and solving problems worth solving.

Some of these are practical utilities. Others are explorations — a dataset I wanted to understand, a dashboard I needed to exist, or a tool I couldn't find anywhere else. This site serves as the front door to all of it.
